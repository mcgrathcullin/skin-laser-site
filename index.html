Meet Your Provider

Kimberly Mack

Cosmetologist & Certified Clinical Laser Technician

Kimberly's passion for beauty and wellness began at just 17, when she started her career as a licensed Cosmetologist. Her love for helping others led her to study Dietetics & Nutrition at Florida International University, where she earned a B.S. degree and became a Licensed Dietitian.

After gaining experience in both skincare and clinical nutrition while living abroad in Singapore, she discovered her true calling in advanced aesthetics. She trained at the world-renowned National Laser Institute, mastering the latest techniques in laser treatments and skin rejuvenation.

Today, Kimberly combines decades of experience in beauty, health, and wellness to create customized treatments that leave clients glowing from the inside out. Her mission is simple: to help you look and feel your personal best, every day.

Licensed Cosmetologist Certified Laser Technician Licensed Dietitian B.S. Florida International

Dr. Amjad Alkadri, MD

Interventional Radiologist & Vein Specialist

Dr. Alkadri brings decades of medical expertise to Skin Laser & MedSpa. After earning his medical degree from the University of Damascus, he completed general surgery and radiology training at Providence Hospital in Michigan, followed by an Interventional Radiology fellowship at the University of Illinois Medical Center in Chicago.

His specialization in minimally invasive procedures and deep understanding of vascular conditions makes him an invaluable part of our team, particularly for laser vein therapy and complex skin treatments.

Board Certified Radiology Interventional Radiology U of I Chicago Fellowship

Yvonne, RN

Registered Nurse & Nurse Injector

Yvonne is a highly skilled nurse injector whose background spans both healthcare and client relations. She brings a rare combination of clinical precision and warm, personalized service to every treatment.

Her commitment to continuous professional development keeps her at the forefront of the latest aesthetic treatments and injection techniques, ensuring every client receives safe, effective, and natural-looking results.
