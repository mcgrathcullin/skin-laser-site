Conditions We Treat

At Skin Laser & Med Spa, we focus on what conditions you are facing, and then recommend the best treatment options for you. We always provide one cost effective & affordable solution, one quick solution, and one long term solution.

Acne & Acne Scarring

Often after vicious acne breakout cycles, many leave behind darker scarring on your face and visible body parts that linger for an extended period of time. While treating your root cause for acne, we help you to remove the scarring that you may currently have. We are also able to treat other scars of any kind and stretch marks.

Treatments: VI Peel, RF Microneedling, Fractional Laser, CO2 Laser

Excess Hair

Tired of spending time and money to remove excess hair from waxing, shaving, and electrolysis? We all have unwanted hair, both male & females, and we can help you remove it.

Treatment: Laser Hair Removal with Cynosure Elite IQ

Learn More

Fine Lines & Wrinkles

Sun exposure and aging break down collagen. CO2 laser, RF microneedling, and fractional laser stimulate new collagen to smooth lines naturally. Many patients say the laser can erase up to a decade in a little less than an hour.

Treatments: CO2 Laser, RF Microneedling, Fractional Laser

Learn More

Hyperpigmentation & Sun Damage

Increased melanocytes or hyperactive melanocytes over produce melanin in certain areas of skin resulting in a brown patch. Most is caused by over exposure from the sun, but some may be caused by hormones and aging. Also includes age spots, brown spots, and freckles.

Treatments: Pico Laser, IPL Photofacial, VI Peel

Learn More

Rosacea & Spider Veins

Spider veins occur in both men and women, usually on legs and ankles, but also other body parts. Laser vein therapy is a non-surgical, non-invasive method to reduce and eliminate spider veins and redness.

Treatments: Laser Vein Therapy, Laser Genesis

Learn More

Large Pores & Uneven Texture

Laser Genesis facial treats oily skin, blackheads, large pores, and acne. Pico laser heats skin's surface, boosts healing, and removes dead skin cells. Good for all skin tones.

Treatments: Laser Genesis, Microneedling

Learn More

Scars & Tattoo Removal

A scarred face can have a psychological effect. Accidents, surgeries, and acne can leave permanent scars. Venus Viva Nano-Fractional ablative RF technology provides resurfacing to even out scars, texture, and discoloration. For tattoos, PicoWay treats the full spectrum of ink colors.

Treatments: RF Microneedling, CO2 Laser, PicoWay Laser

Learn More

Sagging Skin

Sun exposure is the biggest culprit behind most skin laxity. Loose skin can appear anywhere on the body, but mostly is found on the face and jowls area. Our treatments stimulate collagen production to tighten and firm skin naturally.

Treatments: RF Microneedling, IPL Photofacial

Learn More

Stretch Marks

Stretch marks are reddened streaks, sometimes whitish in color, indented, usually appear on skin from pregnancy, weight gain, growth spurts in teens, and rapid muscle gain through weightlifting. Many topical products promise to rid, but don't live up to the hype.

Treatments: RF Microneedling, CO2 Laser
