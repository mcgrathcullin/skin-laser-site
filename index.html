Meet Your Provider

Kimberly Mack

Cosmetologist & Certified Clinical Laser Technician

Kimberly's passion for beauty and wellness began at just 17, when she started her career as a licensed Cosmetologist. Her love for helping others led her to study Dietetics & Nutrition at Florida International University, where she earned a B.S. degree and became a Licensed Dietitian.

After gaining experience in both skincare and clinical nutrition while living abroad in Singapore, she discovered her true calling in advanced aesthetics. She trained at the world-renowned National Laser Institute, mastering the latest techniques in laser treatments and skin rejuvenation.

Today, Kimberly combines decades of experience in beauty, health, and wellness to create customized treatments that leave clients glowing from the inside out. Her mission is simple: to help you look and feel your personal best, every day.
