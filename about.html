Meet Your Provider

Kimberly Mack

Cosmetologist & Certified Clinical Laser Technician

Kimberly's passion for beauty and wellness began at just 17, when she started her career as a licensed Cosmetologist. Her love for helping others led her to study Dietetics & Nutrition at Florida International University, where she earned a B.S. degree and became a Licensed Dietitian.

After gaining experience in both skincare and clinical nutrition while living abroad in Singapore, she discovered her true calling in advanced aesthetics. She trained at the world-renowned National Laser Institute, mastering the latest techniques in laser treatments and skin rejuvenation.

Today, Kimberly combines decades of experience in beauty, health, and wellness to create customized treatments that leave clients glowing from the inside out. Her mission is simple: to help you look and feel your personal best, every day.

Medical Director

Dr. Amjad Alkadri, MD

Interventional Radiologist and Medical Director

Dr. Alkadri received his medical degree from the University of Damascus. He completed his general surgery and radiology training at Providence Hospital in Michigan in 1999, followed by an Interventional Radiology fellowship at the University of Illinois Medical Center in Chicago.

Board Certified in Radiology, Dr. Alkadri brings deep expertise in minimally invasive surgery to our practice, ensuring patients receive the highest standard of care for vein treatments and related procedures.

Nurse Injector / Laser Technician

Yvonne

Registered Nurse / Nurse Injector / Laser Technician

Yvonne is a highly skilled nurse injector and laser technician who brings together a background in general management and customer service with a deep passion for healthcare. She specializes in neuromodulators, dermal fillers, and laser procedures, combining technical expertise with a warm, attentive approach that puts clients at ease.

Committed to continuous professional development, Yvonne stays current with the latest aesthetic treatments and injection techniques, ensuring every client receives safe, effective, and natural-looking results.
