Reveal your best skin with medical-grade peels

At Skin Laser & MedSpa, we offer top-tier facial and body peels designed for long-lasting skin rejuvenation. While at-home exfoliation helps maintain your complexion, our medical-grade, medium-depth peels go far deeper, effectively removing damaged outer layers to improve texture, minimize fine lines, and reduce pore size.

During your free consultation, we will evaluate your skin and determine the best formulation from our five-plus peel options to address your specific concerns. The VI Peel, recognized as the number one chemical peel in the industry, is among our most popular treatments for good reason: it delivers visible, transformative results within a single week.

Facial peels

Our VI Peel facial formulas target the five most common skin concerns: acne, melasma, aging skin, hyperpigmentation, and acne scarring. These solutions work synergistically to combat active symptoms caused by dead and damaged skin lingering on and below the surface. The treatments are generally safe for all skin types on the Fitzpatrick scale, and you can expect visible results within seven days.

Body peels

VI Peel offers a formula specifically designed for the body, targeting smaller areas like the bikini area, knees, elbows, and underarms. This advanced formula is designed for thicker skin, penetrating deeper to address concerns in these targeted zones across all skin tones. Peeling begins 7 to 10 days after treatment and lasts about 3 to 5 days. Fewer treatments are typically needed, and there is more visible peeling. Body peels effectively address hyperpigmentation, uneven texture, acne scarring, and discoloration.

The ZO stimulator peel

For those seeking a quick refresh with zero downtime, our ZO "lunch time" stimulator peel is an excellent option. This treatment exfoliates the superficial layers of your skin, dissolves dead skin cells, and promotes healthy new cell growth. There is no peeling, no redness, only a natural, healthy glow immediately after treatment. This peel can be repeated at two-week intervals for ongoing radiance.

What to expect after your peel

Application of the VI Peel typically takes about 30 minutes. Peeling begins around day three and lasts a few days depending on your skin type and condition. During this time, you can use a moisturizer to soothe the skin and minimize visible peeling. By day seven, you will see visible results with a brighter skin tone and noticeably healthier, smoother texture.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions About VI Peel

How long does a VI Peel take?

Application of the VI Peel typically takes about 30 minutes. Peeling begins around day three and lasts a few days depending on your skin type. By day seven, expect brighter skin tone and noticeably healthier, smoother texture.

What types of VI Peels are available?

We offer facial peels with five-plus formulations targeting acne, melasma, aging skin, hyperpigmentation, and acne scarring. Body peels are available for smaller areas like bikini, knees, elbows, and underarms. The ZO Stimulator Peel is a zero-downtime lunchtime option with no peeling or redness.

Is the VI Peel safe for all skin types?

Yes. The VI Peel is safe for all Fitzpatrick skin types. It is the number one chemical peel in the industry with visible results within one week.

What is the recovery like after a VI Peel?

Peeling begins around day 3 and lasts 3 to 5 days. Use moisturizer to soothe skin and minimize visible peeling during this period. Visible results appear within 7 to 14 days. The ZO Stimulator Peel has zero downtime and can be repeated every two weeks.
