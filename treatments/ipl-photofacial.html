Advanced Light Therapy

IPL Photofacial

Harness the power of intense pulsed light to correct discoloration, reduce redness, and restore a vibrant, even complexion.

Brighter, healthier skin through light-based rejuvenation

IPL Photorejuvenation is an advanced light-based treatment that combines the best features of laser technology and dermatology into one highly effective device. By delivering targeted pulses of broad-spectrum light into the skin, IPL breaks down discoloration and stimulates collagen production, leaving you with noticeably brighter, healthier, and more vibrant skin.

If you experience frequent sun damage, rosacea, or other environmental effects that affect your complexion, the IPL photofacial works exceptionally well at removing discoloration, improving skin texture and tone, and providing a tightening effect to the skin. The treatment is non-invasive with minimal downtime, making it an ideal option for those with active lifestyles.

One of the greatest advantages of IPL Photorejuvenation is its versatility. We can treat many different skin conditions across a wide range of skin tones on the Fitzpatrick scale, making it an accessible solution for virtually anyone looking to improve their complexion and restore their confidence.

Conditions treated

IPL photofacials are highly effective at addressing a wide range of skin concerns, from superficial discoloration to deeper textural irregularities.

Rosacea Spider Veins Sun Damage Age Spots Hyperpigmentation Fine Lines Acne Scarring Facial Capillaries Uneven Tone Premature Aging

Treatment timeline

Each IPL photofacial session typically takes 30 to 35 minutes. You may notice slight redness immediately after treatment, which subsides within a short time. We typically recommend three to five sessions for optimal results, spaced several weeks apart to allow the skin to fully respond between treatments.

To maintain your results long-term, we usually recommend one to two maintenance sessions per year after completing your initial treatment course. This ensures your complexion stays clear, even, and radiant throughout every season.
