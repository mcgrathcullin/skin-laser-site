Turn back the clock on your skin

CO2 laser resurfacing is the gold standard for skin rejuvenation. The procedure works by vaporizing the top layers of damaged skin while simultaneously stimulating new collagen production in the sub-dermal tissue beneath. The result is dramatically smoother, tighter, more youthful skin.

Our Phoenix CO2 Laser system is FDA-cleared and delivers precise energy to target fine lines, wrinkles, sun damage, and scarring, particularly around the delicate areas of the eyes and mouth where aging shows first. The controlled removal of damaged skin triggers your body's natural healing response, producing fresh, healthy tissue and a renewed complexion.

What it treats

  • Fine lines and wrinkles
  • Sun damage and age spots
  • Acne scarring
  • Hyperpigmentation
  • Uneven skin texture
  • Scarring around eyes and mouth
  • Rough or dull skin
  • Loss of skin elasticity

Acne scar reduction

For patients with moderate to severe acne scarring, fractional CO2 laser offers a powerful solution. The fractional approach creates microscopic treatment zones surrounded by untouched skin, which accelerates healing time while still delivering significant improvement in scar depth and texture. Multiple sessions can dramatically transform scarred skin, restoring a smoother, more even surface.

Hand restoration

The hands are one of the first places to show age, yet they are often overlooked. We use the Symmetry Laser by Rohrer Aesthetics for hand rejuvenation, the first FDA-cleared device specifically designed for treating the hands. Powered by AI and SwiftScan technology, it delivers precise energy to reduce age spots, smooth crepey skin, and restore a more youthful appearance to your hands.

What to Expect

Your treatment journey

From your initial consultation through recovery, here is what the CO2 laser experience looks like.

01

Consultation

We assess your skin, discuss your goals, and determine if CO2 resurfacing is the right approach. We will explain what to expect and create a personalized plan.

02

Preparation

A topical numbing cream is applied before treatment for your comfort. The treatment area is cleansed and prepared for the laser.

03

Treatment

The CO2 laser is precisely applied to the treatment area. Most sessions take under an hour, even for full-face resurfacing. You may feel warmth and mild tingling.

04

Recovery

Expect redness and some peeling for 5 to 7 days as new skin emerges. We provide detailed aftercare instructions and are available for any questions during your healing.
