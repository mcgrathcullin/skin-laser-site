How it works

The Pixel8-RF by Rohrer Aesthetics uses radiofrequency energy delivered through precision microneedles to create controlled micro-injuries deep within the skin's surface. This process triggers your body's natural healing response, repairing visible signs of damage while stimulating collagen and elastin production deep within the dermis.

Unlike traditional resurfacing methods, the Pixel8-RF is highly customizable. Your provider adjusts the energy level, needle depth, and coverage area to match your specific skin concerns, making it effective for everything from fine lines to deep scarring.

Enhanced Results

Combined with AnteAGE regenerative science

Every RF Microneedling session at Skin Laser & MedSpa is paired with AnteAGE Regenerative Stem Cell Growth Factors for optimal results. These lab-derived growth factors are delivered directly into the micro-channels created during treatment, accelerating healing and maximizing collagen production.

For patients dealing with hyperpigmentation, we add AnteAGE MD Brightening Solutions, which combines tranexamic acid, N-acetyl glucosamine (NAG), and melanostatin. This targeted formula works synergistically with the RF energy to inhibit melanin overproduction and even out skin tone over successive treatments.

What to expect

1

Arrive early for numbing

Plan to arrive 1 hour prior to your appointment. A topical numbing cream is applied to ensure your comfort throughout the treatment.

2

Customized treatment

Your provider will calibrate the Pixel8-RF settings based on your skin type, concerns, and treatment goals. The radiofrequency energy is delivered precisely through microneedles to targeted areas.

3

Growth factor application

AnteAGE Regenerative Stem Cell Growth Factors are applied immediately after treatment, penetrating deep into the freshly created micro-channels for maximum absorption and repair.

4

Session details & series

Each session takes 30 minutes to 1 hour depending on the treatment area. For optimal results, a series of 3 to 5 treatments is recommended, spaced several weeks apart to allow your skin to heal and rebuild between sessions.
