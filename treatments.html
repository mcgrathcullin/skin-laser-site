Most Popular

Laser Hair Removal

Powered by the Cynosure Elite IQ with dual-wavelength technology and FDA-cleared Melanin Reader, which identifies the optimal settings for every skin type, lifestyle, and ethnicity. Zimmer cooling ensures maximum comfort. Treat the face, chest, back, underarms, legs, bikini, and more.

Explore Laser Hair Removal
Advanced

CO2 Laser

The Phoenix CO2 Laser is FDA cleared for skin resurfacing, acne scarring, hand restoration, and hyperpigmentation. It works by vaporizing the top layers of skin and stimulating new collagen production, treating wrinkles, sun damage, and scarring.

Explore CO2 Laser
Resurfacing

Fractional Laser

Fractional laser creates micro-columns of thermal damage to stimulate collagen remodeling and skin renewal. Effective for acne scarring, fine lines, uneven texture, and sun damage with controlled downtime.

Explore Fractional Laser
Collagen Boost

Microneedling

AnteAGE MD Stem Cell Growth Factor is infused while the pen punctures the epidermis and dermis, promoting natural healing with increased collagen and elastin production. Combined with AnteAGE MD Exosomes for enhanced results.

Explore Microneedling
Pico Laser

Pico Lazer

The Pico laser delivers ultra-short picosecond pulses to break down pigmentation and ink into tiny particles. FDA approved and safe on all skin types. Treats tattoos, hyperpigmentation, age spots, and skin revitalization.

Explore Pico Laser
Rejuvenation

RF Microneedling

The Pixel8-RF by Rohrer Aesthetics delivers radiofrequency energy through precision microneedles, triggering your body's natural repair of visible damage. Treats fine lines, wrinkles, acne scars, and skin laxity.

Explore RF Microneedling
Glow Treatment

Laser Genesis

A laser is held above the skin in a cross-hatching pattern for a generally painless treatment. Boosts collagen and elastin production, removes dead skin cells, and kills acne-causing bacteria. Good for all skin tones with no downtime.

Explore Laser Genesis

Hydrafacial

A multi-step Cleopatra facial treatment that cleanses, exfoliates, extracts, and hydrates the skin. Delivers instant results with no downtime, leaving skin refreshed, radiant, and deeply nourished.

Facials

VI Peels

Carbon Laser Facial Peel

Also known as "The Hollywood Peel," this treatment applies a carbon layer to the skin and uses laser energy to deeply exfoliate, unclog pores, and stimulate collagen for an instantly radiant complexion.

3 IN 1 TriBella Facials

IPL Photo Facial
