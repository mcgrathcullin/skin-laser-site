Treats Anti-Aging

Expert injections for natural results

Our injectable treatments are administered by Yvonne, RN, a skilled and experienced nurse injector with a refined eye for facial aesthetics. Whether you are looking to soften fine lines, smooth deeper wrinkles, or restore lost volume, Yvonne takes a personalized approach to every treatment, ensuring results that look natural and feel like you.

Botox works by temporarily relaxing the muscles responsible for dynamic wrinkles, the lines that form from repeated facial expressions like squinting, frowning, and raising your eyebrows. The result is smoother, more relaxed skin that still allows for natural expression. Results typically appear within 10 to 14 days and last up to 3 to 4 months depending on metabolism.

Dermal fillers take a different approach, using hyaluronic acid-based gels to restore volume, smooth static wrinkles, and enhance facial contours. Fillers are ideal for addressing volume loss in the cheeks, smoothing nasolabial folds (smile lines), enhancing the lips, and softening under-eye hollows. Results are immediate and can last 6 to 18 months depending on the product and treatment area.

What to expect

Both neuromodulator and filler treatments are quick, typically taking 15 to 30 minutes. Yvonne will discuss your goals during a consultation and develop a treatment plan tailored to your facial anatomy and desired outcome. Most patients return to their normal activities immediately after treatment.

Minor redness, swelling, or bruising at the injection site is normal and typically resolves within a day or two. Yvonne uses precise injection techniques and, when appropriate, topical numbing to ensure your comfort throughout the procedure.
